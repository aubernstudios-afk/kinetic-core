PRIVACY POLICY // KINETIC CORE

Last Updated: July 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy outlines how Aubern Studios ("we," "our," or "us") handles data for the mobile application "Kinetic Core" ("the Application"). We are committed to protecting user privacy under UK and EU GDPR frameworks.

2. DATA COLLECTION & USE

The Application collects minimal, pseudonymized technical identifiers strictly required to provide core gameplay and competitive functionality:

  • Unique Profile IDs & Usernames: Generated locally on your device to create a leaderboard profile. This data is securely processed using Supabase cloud infrastructure to sync high scores and cross-device meta-progression.
  • Gameplay Metrics: High scores, sector completions, and currency counts are tracked to update global leaderboards.

3. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES & ADVERTISING

The Application implements Google AdMob to deliver opt-in, rewarded video advertisements. Google AdMob may process device identifiers, advertising IDs, and coarse location data to serve personalized or contextual ad metrics. Where applicable inside the UK and EEA, user consent is collected natively via Google's consent management tools.

4. USER-CONTROLLED DATA DELETION

In strict compliance with GDPR and Google Play data transparency mandates, users maintain complete ownership of their data. A self-service "DELETE DATA & ACCOUNT" button is available inside the Application's Skin Vault menu interface. Activating this feature instantly purges your Profile ID, custom username, and all high-score associations from our live Supabase cloud database, while automatically wiping the local cache on your mobile device.

5. CONTACT

For any questions regarding this compliance statement or data processing inquiries, please contact our system administrator support channel.
